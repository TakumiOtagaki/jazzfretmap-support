# Privacy Policy for JazzFretMap (FretSense)

**Effective Date:** March 6, 2026

## Summary
JazzFretMap (brand: FretSense) is a jazz guitar fretboard drill app with CAGED-based quizzes and study mode. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. All app data stays on your device.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data. The app only stores the following locally on your device:
- App settings
- Practice history

## Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or other identifiers
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or files
- Analytics or usage tracking
- Advertising identifiers

## Third-Party Services
We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## In-App Purchases
We may add a one-time in-app purchase (premium unlock) in the future. Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details.

## Data Storage
All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any server backend for JazzFretMap, and no data is transmitted off-device.

## Children’s Privacy
JazzFretMap is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security
Because we do not collect or transmit data, your information remains on your device. You control your device’s security and access.

## Changes to This Policy
If we make changes, we will update the Effective Date and post the revised policy with the app or on our website if one is added.

## Contact
If you have any questions, contact us at takumi@example.com.
