## FAQ
- What does the app do?
  JazzFretMap trains fretboard awareness with CAGED-based drills. Use Quiz for timed practice and Study to explore shapes without pressure.
- What is the difference between Quiz and Study?
  Quiz mode prompts you to answer and tracks your score. Study mode lets you explore notes and shapes at your own pace.
- What does “R” mean on the fretboard?
  “R” marks the root note for the current shape or key.
- What does CAGED mean?
  CAGED is a system that maps chord shapes (C, A, G, E, D) across the neck to visualize scales and chord tones.
- Why are some frets disabled?
  Some drills limit the fret range to train specific positions. This is called form‑range training.
- How does scoring or submit work (if present)?
  In Quiz mode, your answer is checked when you submit. Correct answers increase your score; incorrect answers do not.
- The app won’t load or seems frozen. What should I do?
  Force‑quit and relaunch the app. If it persists, see Troubleshooting below.
- My settings or progress reset. Why?
  Data is stored on‑device. Reinstalling the app or clearing storage can remove local data.

## Troubleshooting
- Restart the app (force‑quit and reopen).
- Update iOS/macOS to the latest version supported by your device.
- Update JazzFretMap to the latest version.
- Reinstall the app (note: this can reset local data).
- Reset in‑app settings if the app provides a reset option.
